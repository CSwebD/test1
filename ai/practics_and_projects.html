Overview

In this course, you will complete real-world AI projects. You will collect data, train models, evaluate outputs, and document your results.

Instructions

1. Watch each project walkthrough.
2. Rebuild the project yourself.
3. Modify it to make it unique.
4. Upload to GitHub to showcase.

Good luck!
