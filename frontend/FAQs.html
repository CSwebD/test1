Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about how Skill Maps works and how you can make the most of it.

How do I track my progress?

You can create an account on Skill Maps to unlock progress tracking across all roadmaps and skill pathways.

Are the learning pathways free?

Yes — all Skill Maps content is completely free to access for all learners worldwide.

Can I contribute content?

Absolutely! Join our Discord community or contact us to collaborate and share your expertise.

Are there certificates?

We’re developing certification options for key roadmaps and skill challenges — stay tuned for updates.

Who maintains Skill Maps?

Skill Maps is maintained by a team of software engineers, educators, and open-source contributors. Learn more on our About page.
